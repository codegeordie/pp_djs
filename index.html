About

Troy Prutzman and his wife Deb Prutzman, owners of Party Pumpers DJs have seen and done it all, from night clubs and weddings to corporate events and private venues.

Troy a DJ of 20+ years is a master emcee and crowd motivator. He brings a unique high energy experience to fully engage you and your guests. Deb’s exceptional organization skills and attention to detail make every event run smoothly. Working together, they create a professional atmosphere personalized for each and every client.

DJ Party Pumpers is based out of Pen Argyl, Pa. The perfect location between the Lehigh Valley, Poconos and New Jersey. They have even been known to contract events up and down the east coast, from Buffalo to Florida!

Troy and Deb met at a DJ event almost 20 years ago. They are now proud parents of three. One serving in the US Army, another attending a university and one working in the trades.
